Ballot as an Individual

You don't have to be in a syndicate with other people - you can also enter as an individual. To do this, you must create a syndicate with only you as a member.
From your Dashboard, click 'create syndicate', then 'create'. You will be allocated a syndicate number. Nothing more needs to be done - sit back, relax and wait for ballot day.

If you change your mind, and want to be added to somebody's syndicate, it's no problem! Just get in touch through livechat, or email an administrator at internet@jcr.dow.cam.ac.uk. Make sure you include the syndicate number in your message.
