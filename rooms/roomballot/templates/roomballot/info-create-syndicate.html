Creating Your Syndicate

You can feature on the ballot in two ways: either as an individual or as part of a syndicate. A syndicate is a way for a group of people (maximum 6 people) to choose rooms at the same time as each other. If you want to live with a certain group of people next year, being in a syndicate with them is a way to make that more likely because you can easily coordinate what you pick when it's your turn on ballot weekend. Being in a syndicate does not mean that you have to live with the people in it - it merely makes the option of living with them easier.

Once you have decided who you wish to form a syndicate with, you must create your syndicate on the room balloting system. On your Dashboard, click 'create syndicate'. Search for people you want to add to your syndicate. When the person appears, click their name to add them. Note that syndicates can comprise at most 6 people. If you make a mistake, click 'start again' and your selection will be cleared. Once you're happy with your selection, click 'create'. Your syndicate will be created, and you will be allocated a syndicate number. Then, sit back and wait for the invitees to accept their invitations. They will receive an email notification to remind them to accept. Once all invitees have accepted, you will receive an email confirming the syndicate is now complete.
