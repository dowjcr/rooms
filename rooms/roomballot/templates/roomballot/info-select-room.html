Selecting Your Room

Selecting your room is a piece of cake. When it's time to choose your room, navigate to the room you want to pick. Then click 'select this room'. If your selection is successful, you'll see your room on your Dashboard. If you've made a mistake, and want to change your selection, please get in touch through livechat, or email an administrator at internet@jcr.dow.cam.ac.uk.
